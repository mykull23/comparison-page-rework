Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a Brooklyn Bedding mattress last?
A Brooklyn Bedding mattress typically lasts between 8 and 10 years, depending on the model and how it’s cared for. Hybrid models with coils tend to hold their shape and support longer than all-foam designs. Regular rotation and using a solid foundation can help extend the lifespan of your mattress.

Are Brooklyn Bedding mattresses good for hot sleepers?
Yes, Brooklyn Bedding mattresses are a great choice for hot sleepers. Models like the Aurora Luxe and Spartan Hybrid use cooling technologies such as TitanCool™ and copper-infused foams that help regulate body temperature. These materials draw heat away and promote a more comfortable, balanced sleep climate.

What firmness options does Brooklyn Bedding offer?
Brooklyn Bedding offers a variety of firmness levels to suit different sleep styles and preferences. The Aurora Luxe, for example, is available in soft, medium, and firm, while the Plank Firm and Plank Firm Luxe provide extra support for those who prefer a solid surface. Testing different models in person at SleePare Dallas can help you find your perfect feel.

Do Brooklyn Bedding mattresses come with a sleep trial and warranty?
Yes, all Brooklyn Bedding mattresses include a 120-night sleep trial so you can decide if it’s the right fit for you. Each mattress also comes with a 10-year warranty that protects against manufacturing defects and long-term wear.
