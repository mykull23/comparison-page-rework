What Is The Best Way To Care For Your Casper mattress?
Properly caring for a Casper mattress can help you preserve its quality and maintain its lifespan. 

Use a Mattress Protector: Consider using a waterproof and breathable mattress protector to safeguard your Casper mattress from spills, stains, and dust mites. This can also help preserve the mattress warranty.
Rotate Your Mattress: To promote even wear, rotate your Casper mattress every three to six months. This helps prevent body impressions and maintains consistent support.
Avoid Jumping: Refrain from jumping or standing on your Casper mattress, as this can damage the foam layers and affect its structural integrity.
Provide Proper Support: Ensure your Casper mattress is placed on a sturdy, supportive foundation or bed frame. Avoid placing it directly on the floor, as this can restrict airflow and lead to moisture buildup.
How to test a mattress before buying?
For a thorough mattress test, head to a store and spend 10-15 minutes lying in your usual sleep position. Evaluate pressure points, motion isolation, and overall comfort. Take the time to research materials, build quality, and firmness options. Make sure to also take your personal preferences and requirements into account.
