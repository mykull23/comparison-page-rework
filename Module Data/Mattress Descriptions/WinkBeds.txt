WinkBeds Trial Period and Return Policy
When you purchase WinkBeds through SleePare Dallas, you’ll get a 120-night trial period to test your mattress at home. This gives you about four months to see how it feels in your own bedroom. To qualify for an exchange, you’ll need to keep the mattress for at least 30 days to allow your body to adjust.

If it’s not the right fit, you can exchange it once during the trial window. For returns, WinkBeds charges a $100 fee, or you can drop off your mattress at our store free of charge as long as it’s clean, bagged, and undamaged. Our team will walk you through each step so the process is simple and stress-free.
