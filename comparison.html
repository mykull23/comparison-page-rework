Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best type of mattress? +
Most people prefer memory foam or hybrid mattresses for pressure relief and motion isolation. The "best" depends on sleep position and personal preference — that's why trying in-store matters.
What mattress do you recommend for back problems? +
According to chiropractors, hybrid mattresses like Saatva offer excellent lumbar support and spinal alignment. Always consult your doctor for personalized advice.
What is a bed-in-a-box? +
A mattress compressed and rolled into a box for easy shipping. Many top brands like Casper, Leesa, and Purple use this method for convenient delivery.
What is the best mattress for hot sleepers? +
Latex and hybrids with cooling gel layers work best. Helix Birch and Purple's Grid technology are top-rated cooling choices.
How do I get rid of my old mattress? +
Check local regulations. Many cities offer bulk pickup or recycling programs. SleePare also provides guidance in our blog and in-store.
